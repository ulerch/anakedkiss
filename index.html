About

The project A Naked Kiss has its origin in the European cassette scene of the 80s. The music was recorded analogously on a four-track cassette recorder. And since four tracks are usually too few, the music had to be mixed and copied several times. In the end, each resulting copy was unique and exchanged in the scene against other cassettes.

Thanks to technology, the studio is now the laptop. Although this loses some of its charms, the almost unlimited possibilities and professional sound quality make up for it.

However, the objective has remained the same: To trigger emotions by playing a soundtrack to a fictional movie running in your head only.
